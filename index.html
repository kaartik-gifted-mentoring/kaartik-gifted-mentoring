About Kaartik

Kaartik Issar is an AI researcher, Data Scientist, educator, and mentor specializing in the development of gifted and highly curious students.
His academic and professional background includes:
• High Distinction graduate from the University of Toronto
• Completed dual Bachelor's degrees in Computer Science and Statistics in just four years
• Former Teaching Fellow and Teaching Assistant at the University of Toronto
• Industry experience as a Data Scientist and AI Engineer
• Research experience spanning artificial intelligence, machine learning, and computational sciences
• Author of three research publications
• More than 1,000 hours of teaching experience through Varsity Tutors
• Mentored over 20 gifted students across mathematics, computer science, research, entrepreneurship, and advanced problem solving
Long before becoming a mentor, Kaartik was identified as a gifted student himself in Grade 7 and understands many of the challenges gifted learners face: boredom, lack of intellectual challenge, perfectionism, and the desire to explore ideas beyond what is taught in school.

What Gifted Students Need

Gifted students do not simply learn faster. They often learn differently. Many crave complexity, creativity, depth, and intellectual exploration. Traditional tutoring focuses on grades. Gifted students often need mentorship.

Intellectual Challenge

Students are encouraged to tackle ideas that stretch their thinking rather than simply completing curriculum exercises.

Curiosity-Driven Learning

Sessions begin with questions and interests, not textbooks.

Real-World Projects

Students create meaningful work rather than consuming information passively.

What We Explore

Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning
